# Automobile & Electric Vehicles – Task 2

## Project Overview
This project presents a detailed study of Electric Vehicle (EV) powertrain architecture, electric motor technologies, inverter operation, and Battery Management System (BMS).

The objective of this task is to understand the complete energy flow in an EV system and analyze the key components involved in modern electric vehicles.

---

## Topics Covered

• EV Powertrain Architecture and Energy Flow  
• Electric Motor Types (PMSM, Induction Motor, BLDC)  
• Inverter and Motor Controller Working Principle  
• Battery Management System (BMS) Functions  
• EV Battery Capacity and Range Calculation  
• Case Study Analysis (Tata Nexon EV and Tesla Model 3)

---

## Technical Concepts Included

- DC to AC conversion using inverter  
- Torque and speed control in EV motors  
- SOC (State of Charge) monitoring  
- Thermal management and battery safety  
- Basic EV range estimation formula
